Project information

  • Category: Website Development
  • Client: Industrial Services
  • Project date: 15 December, 2021
  • Project URL: www.industrialservices.com.pk

Description:

Industrial Services is a trading company of industrial products. They approached us to redesign and develop their existing website, with the goal of modernizing the site and improving user experience.
Objectives:

  • Improve the overall visual design of the website to better reflect the company's brand and values.
  • Optimize the website for search engines to improve visibility and drive more traffic to the site.
  • Streamline the user experience by improving navigation, reducing load times, and simplifying the site's content structure.
  • Develop a mobile-responsive design to ensure the website was accessible on all devices.

Approach:
Our team began the project by conducting a thorough analysis of the existing website, reviewing user data and analytics, and assessing the site's strengths and weaknesses. From there, we developed a comprehensive plan for redesigning and developing the website.
We worked closely with the client to ensure the new design reflected their brand and values, incorporating new visual elements, colors, and typography. We also streamlined the site's navigation, organizing content into clear categories and making it easier for users to find what they need
